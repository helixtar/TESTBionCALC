About AlbionMarketCalc

Free · Community · Open

What is AlbionMarketCalc?

AlbionMarketCalc is a free, community-built market tool for Albion Online players. It provides real-time item prices across all 6 major cities — Caerleon, Bridgewatch, Martlock, Thetford, Fort Sterling, and Lymhurst.

Our goal is simple: help players make smarter trading decisions without spending time manually checking every city's market.

Features

Market BrowserBrowse 200+ items — weapons, armor, resources, mounts and more — with live prices and item icons.
City ComparisonSee sell prices and buy orders for every item across all 6 cities side-by-side.
Flip FinderAutomatically scans city pairs to find the most profitable buy-low / sell-high opportunities, sorted by profit margin.

Data Source

All market data is provided by the Albion Online Data Project — a community-run, open API that collects real-time market data from players running the data client.

Prices are updated as players scan markets in-game. Data freshness depends on community participation and may vary by item and city.
